## 🎨 Visual Design Elements

### Service Cards

#### Structure
1. **Top Border Accent** - Animated gradient line (electric blue → cyan) that reveals on hover
2. **Icon Wrapper** - 70x70px square with rounded corners, glowing background
3. **Title** - Large, bold headline (1.7rem)
4. **Description** - Compelling, high-stakes narrative
5. **Service List** - Checkmark bullets with feature items
6. **Emphasis Tag** - Italic, highlighted mission-critical message
7. **CTA Button** - Full-width "Learn More" button

#### Hover Effects
- **Lift animation** (translateY -10px)
- **Top border reveal** (scaleX 0 → 1)
- **Enhanced border glow** (electric blue)
- **Background gradient shift**
- **Icon wrapper scale** (1.05x) with enhanced shadow
- **Button color inversion** (transparent → electric blue fill)

### Color & Styling
- **Background**: Graphite (#1a1a1a)
- **Border**: Electric blue with low opacity, increases on hover
- **Icons**: Electric blue (#00d4ff) in glowing wrappers
- **Emphasis tags**: Italic, electric blue text on subtle blue background
- **Buttons**: Transparent → filled on hover with upward lift

### Emphasis Tags (Mission-Critical Messaging)
- **Visual style**: Subtle blue background panel with lock icon
- **Typography**: Italic, medium weight, slightly smaller (0.95rem)
- **Color**: Electric blue (#00d4ff)
- **Purpose**: Reinforce that these services are essential, not optional

### Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop**: 3-column grid

- **Scroll reveal**: Staggered entrance (0.2s delay between cards)
- **Hover states**: Smooth 0.4s transitions
- **Top border**: 0.4s scaleX animation
- **Icon wrapper**: 0.3s transform and shadow
